Welcome to my project portfolio!  
This repository highlights a collection of academic, professional, and side projects built using Python, R, SQL, PySpark, Tableau, Power BI, and Streamlit.  
Each project applies data science, machine learning, and analytical techniques to solve real-world problems.

---

## Project Highlights

### 1. Chicago Taxi Trips Analysis
Analyzed 6.38M Chicago taxi trips across 23 variables using R and Python, applying statistical methods including linear regression, inference, and F-tests. Built interactive visualizations and prediction tools to evaluate trip patterns, durations, and citywide trends.

---

### 2. GenAI-Powered Legal Assistant (LexLead.AI)
Developed an open-source legal AI assistant specialized in Texas Family Law. Incorporated RAG pipelines, fine-tuning, safety alignment, and LLM-as-Judge evaluation, achieving an 80% improvement in accuracy over baseline models.

---

### 3. Receipt Volume Forecasting System
Built an end-to-end forecasting system to predict annual receipt volumes using daily historical data. Implemented Prophet, ARIMA, and XGBoost with cross-validation, feature engineering, and visualization to support revenue forecasting, staffing, and capacity planning.

---

### 4. Amazon Reviews Authenticity Analysis
Leveraged Google Cloud Dataproc and PySpark to analyze ~50GB of Amazon product reviews and detect patterns indicative of fraudulent or AI-generated content. ### 5. Customer Churn Prediction for a Fitness Center
Developed machine learning classifiers (Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Ensemble Models) to predict member churn. Enabled targeted retention strategies and improved customer engagement insights.

---

### 6. McDonald's Sales & Promotion Analysis
Assessed the impact of the All-Day Breakfast promotion using time-series analysis, A/B testing, and dashboarding. Measured effects on revenue growth, repeat visitation, and new customer acquisition.

---

### 7. Breast Cancer Prediction
Built predictive models using SVM, KNN, and Random Forests, with detailed feature engineering and hyperparameter tuning. Emphasized model accuracy, recall, and clinical interpretability for improved decision support.

---

### 8. Patient Readmission Analysis
Performed statistical and predictive analysis to examine hospital readmission drivers. Identified high-impact risk factors to support quality improvement and resource allocation.

---

### 9. Online Gaming Player Engagement Analysis
Developed a player engagement prediction model using gameplay behavior features. Identified key engagement drivers and recommended retention optimization strategies for game designers.
